Discord Webhook

Alongside the Discord Bot I had created, I programmed webhooks that uploaded posts from subreddits to discord server channels through a third-party website. The webhooks programmed in Javascript transformed subreddit posts into embedded discord posts. Learning how to use Javascript was a challenging experience that refined my problem-solving and research skills.
